Bluefish444

TouchDesigner supports Bluefish444 video input and output devices through the Video Device In TOP and Video Device Out TOP.

The KRONOS range is the latest generation of video, audio, and data processing I/O cards from Bluefish444. Built with multi-channel 4K/UHD 60p, High Dynamic Range, and High Frame-Rate SDI to address applications in broadcast, production, post-production, immersive graphics, live events, streaming and more.

The Epoch | 4K Supernova S+ range and Epoch | Supernova CG are multi-channel 3G/HD/SD SDI I/O video cards enables TouchDesigner users to choose between a number of configurable I/O combinations.

The Epoch | Neutron range offers both full height and low profile form factor variants, in either multi-channel 3G/HD or HD/SD with HDMI preview.

See Bluefish444 TouchDesigner page

Bluefish Support in TouchDesigner
[edit]

TouchDesigner has built-in native driver support for Bluefish devices.

Ways to interface with Bluefish444 cards in TouchDesigner

  • Video Device In TOP - Captures video streams from capture cards and input devices.
  • Video Device Out TOP - Outputs video.

The term "Frame" is a measurement of time used (1) in the Timeline, (2) as a time-unit in CHOPs, and (3) as a time unit in movie files that are read into TOPs and written out from TOPs. The frame rate is the frames per second (FPS).
